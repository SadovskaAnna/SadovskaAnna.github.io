About the course

This is a small navigation website for the teachers, who are going to teach English for Medical Puposes with us at egoPERFECTUS. This course and website were prepared by egoPERFECTUS teacher Anna Sadovska. This page will help you to navigate, understand the materials and not get lost in them.

This course has been tried and has received exceptionally good reviews. You are welcome to use the materials inserting your own name and leaving mine (Anna's) as the owner's.

Your contribution

If you have any suggestions or comments, you are more than welcome to contact me, Anna Sadovska, or other teachers who taught EMP at egoPERFECTUS.

Together we can bring the course to perfection!

Useful Links:

TEFLtastic

Medical English games / worksheets

You can find some games to play with your students or useful worksheets. The tasks and games are offered here in PDF format or as a separate website.

liveworksheets.com

A very useful source to find the worksheets on any topic

You can find quite a lot of different interactive worksheets on any topic here, on liveworksheets.com. Allow your students to complete them independently or print them out. Registration is required for PDF format. If you wish, you can also create a new worksheet there. Might be used for testing, too.

TEDMED Talks

Scientific videos on different medical topics

Watching the videos is students' favourite part of the lesson! :) Try to find something controversial to watch as your students might be glad to discuss the topic or hold a debate.

Materials on 'The Human Body'

A useful PDF file

Some tasks in the presentation on 'The Human Body' were taken from here. Some pages could be extracted and distributed among students.

A Grammar Cheatsheet

Grammar only

If your students forgot some grammar topics, this cheatsheet can help them to remember some. You can also give them some extra things to consider, e. g. the list of irregular verbs or a bigger table. However, the students will in most of th cases mostly interested in terminology, not grammar.

Interactive page on human organs

Organ systems

This webpage is just wonderful! It is fun to have a look at the systems, as well as there are great and relatively easy-to-understand descriptions of the human organ systems.
